My Typing Journey

One day in lockdown, I stumbled up on a Ali Abdal youtube channel. And there i saw a typing video of him showing his speed as 140+ , i was stunned out of words. That felt really really cool. Yeah, that's how it started. I saw that video and in that video he suggested how one can start learning typing and gave some necessary website links. so through that i started and practiced every single day, NOt skipped even a single day. And that's my current status in 10fast finers website. I started learning the finger movement from typingmaster website. After that i went to 10fastfingers, as you can see i practiced around 720+ times holding the average of 73wpm and highest 83wpm.
