Top 5 Favourite Series

ATTACKON TITAN / ANIME
When man-eating Titans first appeared 100 years ago, humans found safety behind massive walls that stopped the giants in their tracks. But the safety they have had for so long is threatened when a colossal Titan smashes through the barriers, causing a flood of the giants into what had been the humans' safe zone. During the carnage that follows, soldier Eren Jaeger sees one of the creatures devour his mother, which leads him to vow that he will kill every Titan.
High philosophical series, changes the way you think.

Peaky Blinders
Tommy Shelby, a dangerous man, leads the Peaky Blinders, a gang based in Birmingham. Soon, Chester Campbell, an inspector, decides to nab him and put an end to the criminal activities.Peaky Blinders captures the action, anxieties, exciting highs and deep melancholies that seem to plague the Shelby family as they build a criminal empire comparable to Al Capone's at the height of prohibition.It's the perfect blend of story, acting, dialogues, background music, romance etc. However what makes it stand apart from the others is acting and dialogues. It's got an amazing cast Cillian Murphy, Tom Hardy, Adrian Brody to name a few and Steven Knight to pen down some marvellous quotes.

Game of Thrones
I don't think i need to say anything at all. do I - ^ . oh god, there's something to learn from each and every character. Every character is well developed. If you haven't watched, just go for it already.

Breaking Bad
Walter White, a chemistry teacher, discovers that he has cancer and decides to get into the meth-making business to repay his medical debts. His priorities begin to change when he partners with Jesse. old but totally worth it. You will see how people change.

It' okay not to be okay/ Kdrama
A road to emotional healing opens up for an antisocial children's book author and an employee in a psychiatric hospital. Starting from the really creative look of the show and the way they have incorporated a fairytale to explain the character's feelings and actions in each of the episodes is really new and unique. Right from the first episode, I was pulled in and I really didn't want it to end.HIGHLY RECOMMEND.
