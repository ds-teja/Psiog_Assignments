My Guitar & Piano Journey

There was a time in college when I was walking around and saw someone taking their guitar and I thought to myself "I wish I could learn guitar, but where can I learn, can I spare time for it? will I be able to learn if I buy? oh come on.. I can't" and just left that thought. But in lockdown, without any such thoughts of Where? and How?.. just blindly bought it. And then saw many videos daily, for sure it was hard, but if u love it, it doesn't hurt, does it?
I started watching youtube videos of everything you need to know about guitar since a week before delivery. And read many many articles, made a folder for their bookmarks and took notes of anything i felt important in music theory. Then took further notes of music theory from youtube. And by the time i got guitar i know all the things i should be known.
So I first started learning single string songs from youtube, as you can say i started with happy birthday song on single string and then learned many songs that i like in single string, there were many channels that show videos of single string songs.
And then learned the scales and practiced them all the time such that my finger tips got callouses!! Paaiinnn!! After scales i moved on to two strings an three strings songs, later on moved for other techniques like slides, hammeron an pull offs. Atlast, Finally able to play my favourite songs flawlessly. still a long way to go in strumming. Same process for piano. Just want to say.. if there's anything u love, then just go for it, there's not gonna be any "Right Time". This moment can be the right time.
