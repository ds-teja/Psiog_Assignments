SCHOOL LIFE

School life... I had to shift different schools and so it was difficult to have friends as i keep shifting!! May be that is why i still don't play or watch cricket, haha. So, back in school days, mostly i wouldn't go out to hangout or play outdoor games. I used to watch some shows or Play games by myself, sometimes with kids near the home. Now that i say it, it might sound like i used to alone, but acutally i learned to enjoy my company, for me it's a total bliss to have fun by own sometimes.I believe that everyone should be capable of enjoying their alone time.
And for my 9th, 10th i studied in kkr gowtham international school for which i stayed in hostel. And wow... that first hostel experience is a life changing experience for me. In the initial days i felt home sick all the time. However, i learned how to control that emotions. I used to count days until holidays and i tell myself Only x days. only y days. After a while i fit in with the people and no more counting days. you can say i learned about hope in those days. It changed in many ways by staying in hostel.you know, from getting to see very very different perspectives of people and staying with them!! I understood how we should behave with different types of people and i also got to learn many new things at that school. Like i got addicted to playing chess and then to play Badminton. I used to play chess with my friends even after the lights turned off, by using torch light.haha.And my frined taught me badminton and after daily practice, i was able to beat him in a match, that was one of most satisfying things. I would say, everyone must stay in hostel at sometime, so that they get to discover new things about themselves.

COLLEGE LIFE

It's hostel and in hostel you need a nice group of friends to survive. Gladly, i had my group of firends, we are a very small and silent group. However, we become a beast on sundays. we go to movies, restaurants, arcades and enjoy the sunday to the fullest. sometimes we even go to two movies a day. Those were golden days.

UNIVERSITY LIFE

First year of university was hard to fit in as it is far from our state.!! However, i had a few friends from our college and gladly i used to meet them and worked out fine. Lateron, new batches formed but i couldn't fit in with them too. So i started watched series and english sc-fi movies. And man, i didn't know i would enjoy my own company that much. I totally felt peace and loved watching series/ movies by grabbing some snacks and soft drinks.
In second year the best thing is we went to trip to kodaikanal and that was the best 3 days of my life. Everyone must go for a trip in btech life, seriously it's a must, you will miss out a lot if you don't go.
And as we came from the trip, there was lockdown and that's when i started to change, in lock down i used my time to the fullest, started to learn data science on my own and bought guitar and learned it, learned cardistry, learned piano, learned typing and many more. That helped to learn things on my own and made me a quick self learner. And now i am placed and working in psiog digital
